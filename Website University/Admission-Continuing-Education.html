CONTINUING EDUCATION



DID YOU KNOW?

Every year there are nearly 20,000 enrolments on courses offered by the Department for Continuing Education, making Oxford University one of the largest providers of continuing education in the UK.
Oxford's Department for Continuing Education is also one of the oldest providers of part-time adult education, having been founded in 1878 at the forefront of the University Extension movement.
